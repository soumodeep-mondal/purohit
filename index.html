// New Services

Online Service

Online Puja and different ‘Sanskar’ like ‘Annaprashna’, ‘marriage’ or ‘Shraddha’ that we are successfully conducting from the pandemic situation is started. Now in short here is the procedure to execute the online Puja successfully. First, depending on your particular need like Puja or any other Sanskar Karma, you will receive an appropriate list of materials (Fordo). Second you will carefully go through the list and then simply check out whether the materials are available or not in your locality, or even if it is possible from online. Then we will arrange a meeting, where we will decide alternatives of the unavailable goods according to our rituals. In our Hindu law almost every material has an alternative. Next we will discuss about the Puja utensils which you may already have. From my experience I have seen that normal Puja utensils which a Bengali family have, across the globe is sufficient for Puja needs. If any shortage found out then we will discuss about its correct alternative. Next before your Puja date I will send you a diagram how to prepare yourself as well as how you make the correct arrangements for the purpose. And if require we will discuss several times for perfection. And finally on the designated date with the help of an online platform we both will log in and make the job successful.
